    Rewards Program

    The rewards program is an incentive for you to generate extra income from your investments. We will explain how rewards work below so that you can optimize your earnings. Each reward obtained will be reflected in the table with the exception of the Referral Reward, this one will be automatically awarded once you obtain your referrals.

    Investments

    The first 6 rewards are related to the deposits of each investment. They can only be fulfilled once for each investor. Depending on the amount of the deposit you can obtain a percentage in return. Please find below the distributions and percentages of the investment program:

    • Invest 1 times 15,000 trx or more and get 5% return.
    • Invest 2 times from 7000 trx to 14999 trx and get 4% return.
    • Invest 3 times from 4000 trx to 6999 trx and get 3% return.
    • Invest 4 times from 2000 trx to 3999 trx and get 2% return.
    • Invest 5 times from 1000 trx to 1999 trx and get 1% return.
    • Invest 6 times from 500 trx to 999 trx and get 0.5% return.

    Referrals

    If you get 10 referrals from 1st and 2nd level you will get an extra 1% for each off the 4 levels of referrals. Please see below:

    • 1st Level (5%)
    • 2nd Level (4%)
    • 3rd Level (3%)
    • 4th Level (2%)

    If you get 15 referrals from 1st and 2nd level, a 5th level of referral will be enabled. This allows you to get 1% on your 5th referral level. Please see below:

    • 1st Level (5%)
    • 2nd Level (4%)
    • 3rd Level (3%)
    • 4th Level (2%)
    • 5th Level (1%)

    Reinvest

    You can always reinvest your accumulated earnings. However, you will receive a refund of 5% of the reinvested amount only if it exceeds 40% of the investment amount. For example: If you have an investment of 100 TRX and decides to reinvest retained earnings must exceed 40 TRX to win the 5% reward.

    Do not withdraw

    For each investment plan that ends without withdrawing any profit, you will recive an additional benefit of 15% / 25% / 35% / 55% corresponding to the LIGHT / BASIC / MEDIUM / PREMIUM plans. For example: Your plan will give you 125% of the investment, you would be able to earn 125%+25% in total if you do not withdraw during time of the plan. This reward can be obtained each time you complete a plan. *The REINVEST option does not affect this reward, you can reinvest without losing the extra bonus.

    Daily Bonus

    To achieve this reward you will have to make the highest deposit in one day. The reward would be given every day to the highest investor. This one would be the 10% of your own investment. You will find more information about it at the daily bonus section.

    Best Reward

    To achieve this reward you must accomplish the following requirements:

    • One of the first three rewards on the list (examples: one investment of 15,000 trx or more, two investments between 7000 to 14999 trx or three investments between 4000 to 6999 trx)
    • The two rewards related to referrals.
    • Earn the reward for reinvesting at least once.
    • Do not withdraw during a plan.
    • Be the winner of the BonusDay reward at least once.

    Once these requirements have been accomplished, you will get 30% of all your active investments!
    * Investments that have been already completed their cycle, do not apply to obtain 30% of this reward.

    Daily Bonus

    The timer represents the time remaining to update the next winner of the biggest investment of the day (Daily Bonus). Once the winner is known, it will be reflected in the "Last Daily Bonus Winner" section, the timer will create a new 24-hour cycle where the largest investor of the day will be selected again.
